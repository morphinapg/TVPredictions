This week, ABC renewed 9-1-1 and Grey's Anatomy. Both were predicted as renewals, although Grey's Anatomy was only leaning towards renewal while 9-1-1 was predicted as likely renewed. Either way, 13/13 predictions have been correct so far.

NCIS, So Help Me Todd, FBI: International, and FBI: Most Wanted all drop from likely renewals to just leaning renewed.

Extended Family upgrades from a likely to certain renewal.

The Rookie and CSI: Vegas both drop from leaning towards renewal to leaning canceled.

The Neighborhood upgrades from leaning towards renewal to likely renewed.

The Great North upgrades from a likely cancellation to just leaning canceled.
